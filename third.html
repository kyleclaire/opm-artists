ADIE




Adie Garcia is a Filipino singer, model and social media influencer.

He became known to the public for his viral Youtube videos where he belts out popular songs. Adie Garcia

uploaded videos of his cover songs on Youtube and immediately garnered thousand of views.

In 2020, he signed up as an artist of O/C Records of singer Kean Cipriano. His single Paraluman

was a big hit with more than 3.5 million views on its first month alone. It also made it to No. 1 in the Viral Top-50 on Spotify.

Written and directed by Bela Padilla, the official music video features another social media sensation Ivana Alawi.

In 2021, he became one of the occasional performers of ASAP Natin ‘To, the Sunday afternoon show of ABS-CBN media network.
